What is Good Sleep Quality?

Sleep quality is more than just hours – it’s about how easily you fall and stay asleep, and how rested you feel upon waking. It impacts your sleep cycle and overall well-being. Discover what truly makes a restful night.

How Much Sleep Do You Need?

The right amount of sleep varies by age and lifestyle. Most adults need 6 – 8 hours of sleep, while teenagers may need up to 9. What’s important is waking up energized and ready to take on the day.
